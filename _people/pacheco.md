Working in the field since 2016, his work has led to multiple publications and contributions. Andre has experience in industry as AI Research Specialist at Samsung Electronics, mainly working on the development of new health/well-being algorithms for smartwatches. Currently, he is an Assistant Professor at Federal University of Espirito Santo, where he recently founded the Laboratory of AI for Health.
