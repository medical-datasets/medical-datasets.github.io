description: Leo Joskowicz is a Professor at the School of Computer Science and Engineering at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Israel since 1995. He is the founder and director of the Computer-Aided Surgery and Medical Image Processing Laboratory (CASMIP Lab).  Prof. Joskowicz is a Fellow of the IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers), ASME (Association of Mechanical Engineers), and MICCAI (Medical Image Processing and Computer Aided Intervention) Societies. He has published two books and over 350 technical works including book chapters, conference and journal papers and editorials and has 15 issued patents. He is on the Editorial Boards of several journals, including Medical Image Analysis , IEEE Trans. Medical Imaging , Int. J. of Computer Aided Surgery , and Computer Aided Surgery and has served on numerous related program committees. He is the co-founder of HighRAD, an oncology AI imaging company with FDA-cleared products.
